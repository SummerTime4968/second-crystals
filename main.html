Little Warzone becomes addicting!

A new game on ROBLOX is taking the discord server by storm. The ROBLOX game in question, called Little Warzone, is an all-out fighting game where you play as various insects fighting on top of a baseplate. Players can choose from different types of bugs, such as ants, hornets, dragonflies, spiders, and more, and fight other players that are also insects in the game.

The game, on the time of this publication, ranked up 60,000+ visits and has 2,900+ favorites. It is praised for its simplicity and gameplay, and the game is also compatible with mobile. The controls is also very simple, Left Click for Primary Attack, Double Right Click for the Secondary Attack and the Space-bar is to enable fly for flying insects.

The game however, is also highly addictive as observed in the discord server, as the people in the server are constantly chatting with one another about Little Warzone, and are reported to be frequently playing the game. Even artworks has been made based on the game in the #bird-art channel.
